Sarah McLachlan

Voice of a Generation

Born January 28, 1968, the Canadian singer/songwriter has multiple awards to her name, having sold over 50 million albums worldwide.

With iconic 90s rock ballads such as "Angel," "I Will Remember You," and "Adia" Sarah's music gave a voice to many voiceless 90s teens and young adults like myself, who felt marginalized, or never quite "fit in."

Sarah is perhaps best known for her work in animal rescue and protection, and the founding of the Lilith Fair concert tour, a showcase of all female talent within the music industry at a time when, despite female presence, it was a "boys game" of male-dominated leadership.
